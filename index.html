I’m a self-taught digital product designer who is passionate about creating meaningful user experiences through aiesthetic web and mobile interfaces.

I’m based in Lagos, Nigeria.

I have a background in process flow analysis; in which I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Chemical Engineering from the University of Lagos in Nigeria (First Class Honors - 4.83/5.00 CGPA).

I’m a finalist of the HNG Internship 7.0 (40 designer finalists out of 11,000 participants) where I experienced massive growth among top designers in the world.

I believe design is more of a problem solving activity than an aesthetic activity. With this foundation, my major focus in any digital product design from its inception to the end is the user.

My design process is always hands-on, iterative and transparent.

I focus on creating UI solutions that solve user pain points and eventually increase value and more customers for product companies.
