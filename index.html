My NetWorth

Description:

MyNetWorth helps a user track his/her assets, money and debts to show he/she is rich.

Problems/Challenge:

People (mostly working class individuals) often lose track of their holdings and as a result, their financial health is often at risk.

Activities:

Empathy Map, Market Research, User flow design, UI design.

Results:

The app is built effectively for the user to enable him/her keep track with statistical views, manage his/her holdings and get rewarded with coins as he/she progress through levels. UX creativity was majorly used to provide the user with a wonderful experience after using the app.

BeSilent

Description:

BeSilent automatically puts your phone on silent-mode based on your location, especially during meetings, interviews or in office.

Problems/Challenge:

Ever misssed out on a job just because your phone rang at the most awkward situations? What about on a service day when the sermon is going on but your phone rings when everywhere is calm? Pretty embarassing

Activities:

Market Research, Empathy Map, Information Architecture, User flow design, UI design,

Results:

Using a clear and simple user interface, the user can interact very well with the app. Major features of the app include: Phone auto-silent mode using noise analysis, syncing calendar events using personal emails and silent mode based on set locations. The app is pretty straightforward to use and solves the direct problem of the user.
